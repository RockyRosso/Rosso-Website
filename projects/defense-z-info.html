Defense Z is an up-coming Roblox Tower Defense game being worked on by Runn Studios. The Project is being worked on by Runeraway, MeetDaDawg, RockyRosso, SirNubless, Peenrag6_z9, duccgoquacc, and Rabiolied.

At the moment, there is very little information on the game. As the game gets closer to being finished, more information will be rolled out about it. You can join the Runn Studios Roblox group here.
