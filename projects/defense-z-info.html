Gates of Locus Information

Gates of Locus is an up-coming Roblox game being worked on by Rats United Gamenights. The team that is working on the project is X_Advent, RockyRosso, MasonTheScientistRat, brandonstick, Ulitvi11, condaser, SleepyTufo, LazerPuffin64, zyqrg, and skAter_zebb.

The game is planned to be hosted on a gamenight for the Roblox group known as Rats United. The game at the moment is still in production and no real info has been released on it yet.
